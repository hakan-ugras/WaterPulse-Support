# WaterPulse Privacy Policy


**Last Updated: December 18, 2024**


## Introduction


Welcome to WaterPulse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how WaterPulse handles your information when you use our mobile application.


## Information We Collect


### Personal Information You Provide

When you use WaterPulse, you may provide the following information:


**During Onboarding:**

- Weight (in kilograms)

- Age

- Sex (Male/Female)

- Activity Level (Sedentary/Moderate/Active)


**During App Usage:**

- Water intake logs (amount and drink type)

- Hydration goals and preferences

- Notification preferences and timing

- App usage patterns and settings


### Automatically Collected Information

WaterPulse does **NOT** automatically collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers. We do not use any third-party analytics services or tracking technologies.


## How We Use Your Information


All information you provide to WaterPulse is used exclusively to:


1. **Calculate Your Daily Hydration Goals:** Using scientific formulas based on your weight, age, sex, and activity level

2. **Track Your Progress:** Recording your daily water intake and progress toward your goals

3. **Send Smart Notifications:** Providing personalized reminders based on your preferences and progress

4. **Display Your History:** Showing your hydration patterns in calendar and chart formats

5. **Customize Your Experience:** Personalizing the app interface and settings to your preferences


## Data Storage and Security


### Local Storage Only

**All your personal information and hydration data is stored exclusively on your device.** WaterPulse does not:

- Upload your data to external servers

- Share your information with third parties

- Sync your data to cloud services

- Transmit your personal information over the internet


### Data Security

Your data is protected by:

- iOS built-in security features and encryption

- App sandboxing that prevents other apps from accessing your data

- No network transmission of personal information

- Local database encryption through iOS data protection


## Data Sharing and Disclosure


**WaterPulse does not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to any third parties.** Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no data sharing with:

- Advertising networks

- Analytics companies

- Marketing platforms

- Social media networks

- Government agencies (unless legally required and technically possible)


## Your Privacy Rights and Controls


You have complete control over your data:


### Access and Portability

- View all your data within the app's History and Settings sections

- Export your data through the app's export feature (if implemented)

- Your data remains accessible as long as the app is installed


### Modification and Deletion

- Update your profile information at any time in Settings

- Delete individual water entries from your history

- Completely remove all data by deleting the app from your device


### Notification Controls

- Enable or disable notifications entirely

- Customize notification timing and frequency

- Control notification content and actions


## Data Retention


- Your data is retained only as long as the app is installed on your device

- Deleting the app permanently removes all associated data

- No data backups are created outside your device

- You can manually clear your data history within the app


## Children's Privacy


WaterPulse is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the user's device, parents maintain complete control over their children's data by controlling device access.
