Frequently Asked Questions

How is my daily hydration goal calculated?
Your goal is calculated using a scientific formula: 35ml per kg of body weight, adjusted for age (+10% under 18, -10% over 65), sex (-5% for females), and activity level (1.0x sedentary, 1.15x moderate, 1.3x active). Goals are kept within a safe range of 1500ml - 4000ml.
Why aren't I receiving notifications?
Check that notifications are enabled in Settings > Notifications. WaterPulse also pauses reminders when you've reached your daily goal. If notifications stopped working, try toggling the notification setting off and on again in the app.
How do the smart notifications work?
WaterPulse automatically schedules 30 days of notifications and renews them every 4 hours. The app pauses reminders when you reach your goal and sends milestone celebration notifications at 25%, 50%, and 75% progress.
Can I track different types of drinks?
Yes! WaterPulse supports various drink types including water, tea, coffee, and juice. Each drink type has a hydration multiplier that affects your effective hydration progress.
How do I change my notification schedule?
Go to Settings in the app to customize your reminder intervals (1-8 hours) and set your active hours. You can choose when you want to start and stop receiving reminders each day.
Can I update my personal information and goals?
Absolutely! Visit the Settings tab to update your weight, age, sex, activity level, and other personal information. Your daily goal will automatically recalculate based on the new information.
What do the colors in the calendar mean?
The calendar uses a heat map visualization where darker colors indicate days with higher hydration achievement. Light colors show days with lower intake, while the darkest shade represents days where you exceeded your goal.

Troubleshooting

Notifications Not Working

  1. Check that notifications are enabled for WaterPulse in iOS Settings > Notifications
  2. Verify that Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications
  3. In the WaterPulse app, go to Settings and toggle notifications off, then back on
  4. Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening
  5. If issues persist, restart your device

App Performance Issues

  1. Close other apps running in the background
  2. Restart WaterPulse by closing and reopening the app
  3. Ensure you have the latest version of WaterPulse from the App Store
  4. Restart your device if problems continue

Data Not Syncing

  1. Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  2. Check that WaterPulse has permission to access cellular data in iOS Settings
  3. Force close and reopen the app
  4. Log a water entry to trigger a sync

Privacy & Data

WaterPulse takes your privacy seriously. All your personal information and hydration data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information with third parties.

What Data We Store

  • Personal information (weight, age, sex, activity level) for goal calculation
  • Daily water intake entries and drink types
  • Notification preferences and schedules
  • Historical hydration data for calendar visualization

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

System Requirements

  • Storage: Minimal storage required (under 50MB)
